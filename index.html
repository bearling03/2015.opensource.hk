What is HKOSC?

Hong Kong Open Source Conference is an annual international open source conference in Hong Kong. It is aimed to demonstrate the use of open source software technology, open hardware technology and latest development in the world.

History of Conference

Idea of organizing a open source conference in Hong Kong by community is started in 2004.

After a success in organizing GNOME.Asia Summit 2012 in Hong Kong, open source community organized first community driven open source conference in Hong Kong in 2013.

HKOSC 2015 is organized by Hong Kong Creative Open Technology Association (HKCOTA) which is a non-profit tax-exempted charity organization in Hong Kong.

Hong Kong Linux User Group (HKLUG) and Open Source Hong Kong (OSHK) are co-organizers of HKOSC 2015.
